## Why this kit exists separately from Part 5 / Part 6

Part 5 (*The Second Cairn*) and Part 6 (*Memory Privacy*) are event-anchored — "we shipped a thing, here's what happened." Part 4 is project-anchored — "this is what the project is and why it exists." Different rhetorical shape; different opening line; different ask of the reader. Sharing Part 5 to a community that has never heard of AgentCivics asks them to care about a milestone before they care about the project. Part 4 first, then the others, lands better.
